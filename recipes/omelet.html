A basic tuna breakfast omelet

Description

This is my favorite thing to have for breakfast, and the first thing I learned how to cook period.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/3rd of a can of tuna

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs, and whisk them
  2. Add the tuna and salt and pepper, and mix
  3. Heat up your pan, making sure oil is spread around it
  4. Add in the tuna+egg mixture
  5. Wait until the bottom is a golden brown, and flip
  6. When the other side is finished, your omlet is ready!

And done! You made yourself my favorite breakfast meal
