A basic but tasty smoothie

Description

This is a nice and calm smoothie for when you're craving something easy, healthy, and tasty

Ingredients

  • Fruits
  • Liquid (milk or water)
  • A thickener, like greek yogurt, almond butter, or beanut butter

Steps

  1. Put the fruit in a blender
  2. Then add the liquid
  3. Next, add the thickener to your mixture
  4. Now, rev up that blender! Keep going until its thick and smooth, and take care to get it like that ASAP! Use high speed
  5. Pour and serve!

And done! You made yourself a nice and refreshing smoothie
