Beef fried rice

Description

This was the first real meal I'd cooked for myself, and it was really good and made me happy because it tasted so good, it was healthy, and I didn't have to spend $15 on takeout like I normally would :) Its not that hard to make either! I like to sometimes switch the rice out for noodles to change it up

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lbs of Ground beef
  • Rice or noodles
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • A quarter onion
  • As much (or little) brocolli as you want!

Steps

  1. Start by chopping your onions, and parboiling the brocolli. You can also boil the rice now, though I think its better with day old rice.
